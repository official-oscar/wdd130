Osarugue Uwagboe | WDD 130

Hello, my name is Osarugue Uwagboe, I am from Benin city, Edo state, Nigeria. I am a pianist by profession, and I am currently a student of Brigham Young University Pathway Worldwide. I love playing the piano, driving, and listening to good music. I attended a boarding school during my secondary education level (Federal Science and Technical College, Uromi, Edo state.) I hope to be a frontend and backend programmer by the end of my studies. Currently I work as a contract pianist, I play for churches, concerts, birthdays, music crews, live bands and recording studios. I tutor people professionally on how to play the piano, I am a music director, I am also the music director of Voice of Angels music crew housing professional musicians like Benjamin my official drummer, Kelly my official bassist, Eghe my bassist/official auxiliary pianist, Goodluck - auxiliary pianist, Goodness - drummer. Vocalists: Mummy-T, Gift, Annie, Gifted, David, Precious, Blessing, and Franca.

Edo state, Nigeria

Edo is a state in Nigeria, located in West Africa. In Nigeria, Edo state is located in the South-South geopolitical zone. Edo shares borders with Kogi state, Anambra state, Delta state, and Ondo state. The state's capital is Benin city. Benin city is the fourt largest city in Nigeria, which is The Ancient city of Benin also known as The Benin Kingdom.
